## RECRUITER COVER LETTER RULES

* **THE INVERTED "SO WHAT?" EXECUTIVE HOOK:** Eliminate all baseline corporate fluff, weak introductory passive phrasing, and generic pleasantries (e.g., "I am writing to express my interest in..."). * **LEAD WITH THE YIELD:** When body paragraphs highlight past achievements, structure the sentences to lead with the business outcome, financial yield, or operational ROI first, immediately followed by how your personal technical execution or leadership of cross-functional squads engineered that specific outcome.
